About Xiaoyu Industrial

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Expertise

Guangzhou Xiaoyu Import and Export Co., Ltd. was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between global industrial brands and buyers who need reliable, authentic components — fast.

Based in Nansha District, Guangzhou — one of China's premier logistics and trade hubs — we leverage our location and decade-long relationships with authorized distributors worldwide to source genuine components across 500+ international brands.

Whether you need a single PLC unit or a full project BOM with 500 line items, we consolidate your sourcing into one shipment, one contact, and one streamlined process.

  • 🌐
    Global Sourcing Network

    Direct access to authorized distributors in Europe, North America, Japan, and across Asia — ensuring authentic products at competitive prices.

  • 🛒
    One-Stop Procurement

    Consolidate your entire BOM — multiple brands, multiple categories — into a single order with unified shipping and documentation.

  • 📦
    10+ Years Export Experience

    Expertise in export compliance, customs documentation, HS code classification, and international logistics to 50+ countries.

  • 🏷️
    Multi-Brand Supply

    From Siemens and ABB to niche European brands — we source the exact brand your engineers specify, no substitutions without approval.

  • ⚡
    Fast Quotation

    Standard quotation within 24 hours. Urgent requests handled same-day. Our team works across time zones to minimize your downtime.
